Lake Darby, OH Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Lake Darby is located in western Franklin County at 39°57′42″N 83°13′59″W (39.961630, -83.233059). The majority of the community is in Prairie Township, with two portions extending north into Brown Township. Big Darby Creek, a tributary of the Scioto River, forms the western border of the CDP as well as the Franklin County/Madison County line. The village of West Jefferson lies directly across the creek from Lake Darby.

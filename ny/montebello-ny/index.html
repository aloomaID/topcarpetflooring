Montebello, NY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Montebello (Italian: 'beautiful mountain') is an incorporated village in Ramapo, Rockland County, New York, United States. It is located north of Suffern, east of Hillburn, south of Wesley Hills, and west of Airmont. The population was 4,507 at the 2020 census.
